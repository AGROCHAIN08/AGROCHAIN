Users & Their Roles

Empowering the key stakeholders in the agricultural supply chain: Farmers, Dealers, and Retailers.

Farmers

Primary Producers

List agricultural products, manage inventory, and track sales through personalized dashboards. They can respond to orders and set product prices.

Dealers

Intermediary Buyers

Purchase in bulk from farmers and handle transportation. They can browse products, place bulk orders, and manage distribution to retailers efficiently.

Retailers

End-Point Businesses

Purchase products from dealers for consumer sales. They can place orders, manage inventory, and track deliveries through an integrated system.
